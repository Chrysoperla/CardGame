You are a general of an army in a city-state that wages war with a neighbour city-state. The power of your kingdom is determined by the height of your tower. You can compare it to health points. The tower is protected by wall that takes on itself all the attacks that would otherwise damage the tower.

In order to lead your army to victory, first you have to recruit some mercenary. The list of available troops is the 5 cards in your hand. They can have plenty of different effects, from attacking your enemy to (re)building your own tower. However, no mercenary would follow you for free. You have to pay them in resources they have most interest in. And so humans might be more interested in GOLD, beasts in FOOD and magic adepts in some MANA needed to cast their spells. And so, every turn you get GOLD equal to your MINE level, the more FARM the more FOOD you get and MANA regenerates thanks to magic FOUNTAIN. The cost of hiring a mercenary is written at the bottom of the card and the resources needed are presented by the card color.

    You can achieve victory in 3 ways:
  • Destroy your opponent's tower
  • Build a tower high enough to terrify your enemy and make them surrender
  • Collect so many resources that your enemy gives up

So get ready and lead you army to win. For the kingdom!
